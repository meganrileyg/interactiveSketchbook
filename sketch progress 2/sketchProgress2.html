Micro-Project: Initial Sketch 1

Hesitation

The phone is ringing!

Click the "]" key to reach for the phone.

Currently, the circle is set to expand as the "phone" rings. The main variable is the action keyPressed "]" which triggers the circle to grow.

The pressure is accumulative. The circle will continue to grow as the "phone" rings, and the user continues to press the "]" key. Pressure could be additionally added by introducing a random "drop call" that triggers the circle to restart, effectively delaying the user from "answering" the phone.

The newest addition is the action of "hanging up" the phone. The user can press the "[" key to hang up. If the circle fills the entire screen, the phone is answered. The user then must wait 10 seconds until the "conversation" is over, then the system will automatically reset.
